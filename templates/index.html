keyword scanner

This app scans documents for the "DEI terms" listed in the 2024 report "DEI: Division. Extremism. Ideology: How the Biden-Harris NSF Politicized Science" by the U.S. Senate Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation.

Here are three must-know things about the app (click to expand)
  1. It only detects the exact terms listed in the Appendix B of the report and does not consider the context in which words and phrases are used. For detailed information about what the app does (and doesnâ€™t do), please refer to its GitHub repository.
  2. It supports .txt , .docx, .csv, .xls, .xlsx, and .pdf files and works best with small documents and file sizes (a few pages and MBs). Scanned PDFs and image files are not supported yet. Processing large files may take longer or cause the app to crash.
  3. It runs on a free-tier plan of the hosting server and was not built for production or commercial use. Because of that, it may be slow to load and respond, go offline after a few minutes of inactivity, or stop working if usage limits are reached. If you need faster and more reliable performance, consider running the app on your local machine or deploying it on your own server. For general instructions on creating your own keyword scanner, please see HERE. Lastly, if you have any questions about the app, please reach out via the Issues tab on GitHub.

To get started, simply drag and drop your files or click to select them, then click the scan button.
