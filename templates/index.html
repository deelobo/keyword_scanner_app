keyword scanner

This app scans documents for the "DEI terms" listed in the 2024 report "DEI: Division. Extremism. Ideology: How the Biden-Harris NSF Politicized Science" by the U.S. Senate Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation.

Here are five must-know things about the app (click to expand)
  1. It only detects the exact terms as they are appear in the Appendix B of the report. It will not match a specific term if that term is misspelled in your document. It also does not consider the context in which words and phrases are used.
  2. It supports .txt , .docx, .csv, .xls, .xlsx, and .pdf files and multiple files can be uploaded at once. Scanned PDFs and image files are not supported. The app works best with small documents and file sizes (a few pages and MBs). Processing large files may take longer or cause the app to crash.
  3. It produces two reports in spreadsheet format: Keyword Match Summary Report: Lists the matched terms, the total number of matched terms, and the frequency of each term. Excerpts Report: Contains the matched keywords paired with the sentences in which they appear.
  4. It runs on a free instance of the hosting server and was NOT developed for production or commercial use.
